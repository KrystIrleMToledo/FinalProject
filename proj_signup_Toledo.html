This is a Nationwide Campaign for Clean and Safe Water Access

In the Philippines, millions of families still live without reliable access to clean water. The “Tubig sa Lahat!” campaign is a nationwide call to action that is aiming to spotlight under-served communities, raise awareness, and demand real change.
