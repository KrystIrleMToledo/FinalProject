Why stop water pollution?

Description

This is a website dedicated to educating and inspiring individuals to take action against water pollution. It provides information on the causes, effects, and solutions to water pollution, with a focus on the role of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The website features interactive content, success stories, and practical tips for individuals and communities to reduce their impact on water pollution.

How to solve it?

There are different ways to prevent water pollution from happening that could be a great contribution to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG's). First solution would be, for the government to implement strict rules to people who uses or touch the marine lives such as corals. Second, investing and helping people improve their maintenance towards the water to clean up faster and help more areas. Lastly would be spreading awareness and joining cleanup assembly by different organizations to help and pursue people into helping and be advocates.
